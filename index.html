About Rotaract Club Of TIT & Science, RID 3040

We are the Rotaract Club of TIT & Science, Bhopal — a student-led initiative dedicated to service, leadership, and growth. Through impactful events and community programs, we strive to create change that matters. “Serve. Lead. Inspire.”

Rotaract: Empowering Leaders, Creating Impact

Rotaract provides opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, professional networking, and community service, empowering individuals to make a meaningful impact while building lifelong skills and connections.

What our Director says

It gives me immense pride to witness the remarkable journey of the Rotaract Club of TIT & Science, Bhopal. Since its establishment in 2018, the club has consistently demonstrated the power of student-led initiatives in shaping not just individuals, but communities.

This website stands as a testament to the creativity, dedication, and leadership of our young minds. It not only showcases their achievements but also opens the door for greater outreach, collaboration, and impact.

I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the entire Rotaract team for building a strong platform that reflects the spirit of service, growth, and innovation. May this digital presence continue to inspire many more to join the movement of positive change.
