About RAC TIT & Science RID 3040

The Rotaract Club of TIT & Science is an institution-based international service club affiliated with the Technocrats Institute of Technology and Science in Bhopal, India. Sponsored by the Rotary Club of East Bhopal, it was chartered in 2018. The club operates under Rotary International District (RID) 3040 and embraces the motto "Learn, Achieve, Grow."
  • Established in 2018, the club was formed to empower young leaders through service, fellowship, and professional growth.
  • It was established under the guidance of Dr. Shashi Jain, who was the Director of TIT & Science at the time.
  • The club is sponsored by the Rotary Club of East Bhopal and operates under Rotary International District 3040.
  • Rtr. Wassif Khan served as the first president during Rotary Year (RY) 2018-19, laying the foundation for impactful community service initiatives.
  • The club has since grown, organizing various projects focused on social welfare, leadership development, and global networking opportunities.
