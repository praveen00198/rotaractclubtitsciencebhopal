The Rotaract Club of TIT & Science is an institution-based international services club

Established in 2018 under the guidance of visionary leaders who helped shape the Rotaract movement at TIT & Science…

The club is sponsored by the Rotary Club of East Bhopal and operates under Rotary International District 3040.

It was established under the guidance of Dr. Shashi Jain, who was the Director of TIT & Science at the time.

Rtr. Wassif Khan served as the first president during Rotary Year (RY) 2018-19

Astitva – meaning Existence or Identity – is not just our theme for the year, but a vision to empower every member to discover their purpose, lead with compassion, and create a meaningful difference in society.

Mission

To inspire purposeful service rooted in compassion, cultivate transformational leadership among young minds, create a lasting and meaningful impact on society, and plant seeds of innovation and change that will continue to grow, empowering future generations of Rotaractors from TIT & Science and beyond

Vision

To build a vibrant community of young leaders at Rotaract Club of TIT & Science, dedicated to selfless service, lifelong learning, and creating innovative solutions that shape a better tomorrow.
