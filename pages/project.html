Aashitah : Food Distribution Drive

Aashitah a food distribution drive embodied the spirit of compassion and service. The Rotaract Club of TIT & Science Bhopal RID 3040, continues to uphold its mission of service, fellowship, and leadership, and "Aashitah" is yet another testament to their unwavering dedication towards social causes.

Tarupan : Tree Plantation Drive

Tarupan focused on plantation, sustainability, and giving back to nature. In the event, members and volunteers gathered to plant saplings, water plants, and raise awareness about the importance of greenery and environmental conservation. It reflected RAC TIT & Science's commitment towards sustainable development and community service.

Matritva : Sanitary Distribution Drive

"Matritav" is a dedicated drive to distribute sanitary pads and conduct an awareness session on menstrual hygiene. This initiative is a part of the club’s ongoing efforts to promote health equity and improve the quality of life for women in need.

Kambal Rahat Ka : Blanket Donation Drive

Kambal Rahat was a blanket donation drive organized to help the needy during the harsh winters. The word "Kambal" means blanket and "Raht" means relief, so the event aimed at providing warmth and comfort to underprivileged people who struggle to survive cold nights.
